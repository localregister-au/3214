Part of the [LocalRegister](https://localregister.org) network. See the [Founding Document](https://localregister.org/FOUNDING.md) for the principles this register operates under.

---

## What this is

LocalRegister is not a complaints page and not a campaign. It's a record — a place where what residents of 3214 raise, with councils, ministers, and agencies, gets carried back intact and kept, instead of disappearing the way these conversations usually do.

Most consultation *digests* what a community says — summarises it, processes it, moves on. LocalRegister *registers* it — keeps it whole, dated, and checkable, permanently.

## Issues spanning multiple postcodes

If a concern affects 3214 and other postcodes — the refinery, the gas terminal, regional transport — it's still entered here if 3214 residents raised it, tagged with every postcode it affects. See the entry template in [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md).

---

## What this isn't

Not government-run. Not party-affiliated. Not advocating for any particular outcome.

LocalRegister is pre-political — it records what is happening, not what should happen. What the community does with that record is up to the community.
